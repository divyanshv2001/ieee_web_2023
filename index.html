About The Event

The objective of ICCAMS 2023 is to provide platform to present and exchange the latest research and the corresponding results of Researchers - preferably students, post-graduate students, PhD Research Scholars, Industry Professionals and Post-doc scientists working in the field related to Computer Science & Engineering, Information Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Robotics and Automation. This conference features paper presentations as well as keynote address by Expert Speakers who will focus on recent state-of-the-art technologies in the related areas of the conference. Interdisciplinary research, a major challenge for researchers and academicians in the emerging research world will get more attention. The expected outcome of this international conference are:

  • To establish a networking platform for young researchers, faculties, and experts to share their ideas and solutions in the areas of Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Automation, Cyber Security and Robotics.
  • Provides an opportunity for the young researchers to present their creative and innovative ideas in the areas of Business Intelligence, Ambient Technology, Communication and Latest Technology Trends.
  • Encourages discussion and Networking between the researchers in all the mentioned disciplines.
  • Encourages the Culture of Inter-disciplinary research among the industrialist, Academicians, and Research Scholars. The five major trending areas focused in the conference are Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Ambient Technology, Communication, Business Intelligence and Technology Trends. The conference will facilitate the Industrialist, Research Scholars, and Research associations especially, those, who are carrying out their research work in the aforesaid domain of Computer Science, Information Technology, ECE, EE and Management with valuable discussions in order to make the outcomes more realistic.
